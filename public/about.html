About Us

Uni Education Link

Uni Education Link Tanzania is the best international student recruitment agencies in Tanzania. Many of our directors are alumni from various universities abroad. They have firsthand experience on student's life abroad. For the last three years, our agency has been able to serve more than 2000 students, who benefited from our various programs. Our service-oriented attitude towards study abroad to all our students is exemplary.

Why Choose Us?

We are always engaged and have experience in admission processing, visa attainment, career guidance and counselling, preparation and arrangement of all document required, booking of flight ticket, airport escorts and airport pickup upon arrivals and we are working with different universities in mentioned countries, with affordable expenses. we are recognized by all responsible authorities in Tanzania as well as the universities we are working with globally.

Mission

We make sure we touch every life of Tanzanian who is ready and interested to have quality education from abroad at a minimum cost with partial scholarships.

Vision

To make Tanzania have highly educated people with skills and knowledge ready to contribute to Tanzania's growth and development hence put Tanzania to the next world economic development.
